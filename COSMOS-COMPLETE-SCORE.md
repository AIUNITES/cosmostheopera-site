| Symbol | Name | Description |
|--------|------|-------------|
| [T] | Thyroid | Vibration in thyroid area |

### Degree Indicators
- **+** = slight
- **++** = moderate  
- **+++** = strong
- **-** = decreased
- Numbers 1-5 for precise gradation
