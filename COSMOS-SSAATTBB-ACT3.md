  Horns 1-2: Warm sustained 5ths

[B] MEASURES 33-48: GRAVITATIONAL PULL

  Two violins spiral closer
  Tempo accelerates slightly: ♩= 76 → 84
  Dynamic builds: mf < f

  Sustained, vibrato
