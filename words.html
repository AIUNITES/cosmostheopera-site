THE WORDS

prose poetry from the cosmos

by Tom Sans · 2002–2026

◆ words by Claude · 2026

Act I

We Come

do you see

what do you see when you close your eyes
some don't see they sleep
what i see is the light
an infinite number of stars swirling around in a cloud
some see the dark i see the light
some say it is the snow captured by the vacuum that they see what is it
the echo of all from the start
what do you see when you close your eyes
do you see the dark
i see the light and the stars i see the universe
· · ·

look

look as far as you can see
this life is a journey both endless and so brief both finite and with infinity
you can only see as far as your eyes can perceive
you can only hear the waves that reach the ear
you can only feel as much as your heart can bleed
for a time
i have found the greatest way to see is in the eye of my mind
it can see through all space and time through this journey that is mine
i hope you find your eye you can only look as far as you can see
· · ·

a window

my face is now a window in this world
that hangs here for a place to hold these eyes and peer
my face that hangs here
and looks at you and wonders
if you know where i am and if i am here and if i am there
a mirror in a face that hangs here like a mirror in a frame looking
for you
· · ·

stand

stand still for a moment take a breath and plant yourself look around and notice
you are perched on a rock screaming through space spinning in a spiral around a ball of fire all of us held together by a force invisible
think about this next time you stand STAND
· · ·

i sing

i sing to your soul we want to be free we want to see beauty we want to feel peace we want to be in love we want to be
we want to be free free to love
free to see free to feel
we want to be and we will
be
we only want to see beauty
all that there is all around us
we want to feel the peace all around us
it is time now for a new world
a new world of love
and only love there is only
one
that gives you enough love
we want to be all that we can be in every way we can see we are free
· · ·

we come

we come into being we are beautiful we are lovely we are infinite
we touch we feel we share
we take you there
our peace will fill you
and find you whole
· · ·

journey

the journey of life is not a straight line it twists and turns like the path of a river sometimes calm sometimes rapid
with rocks to teach us to be flexible and strong down waterfalls into still lakes reflecting all that is above joining other rivers to form streams of people who are much like you and me
this journey will end like the rivers run to sea so make the best and drink it in each precious drop, live it freely
· · ·

together

together we come from the echoes of stars together we’ll go to the farthest of far together we are stronger than one shining the light of our love like the sun
together we will last together we breathe together we dream together we love together we BE
TOGETHER!

Act II

Into

the colors

see the colors of the light
in so many ways in so many shades
bending in so many waves
see the light it is like looking in
the windows of a prism in the middle of your mind
so high in the sky somewhere between space and time
see the light in your eye
the light of your life
the colors of the light which shade will it glow
the colors of the light which way will it go
see the colors all around the colors in so many ways just to gaze
to see you in the colors the colors so
beautiful
the colors so alive the colors of your
love
the colors all around in so many ways just to gaze
to see you in the colors the colors so beautiful
the colors so alive the colors of your
love
to see you in the colors the colors so
beautiful
the colors so alive the colors of your
love
see the light how it shines
how it bends how it reflects
in so many shapes and colors
everything you do i am looking at you
i can see the colors of you
· · ·

what are we

what are we
beings of light dressed in skin flames of thought ablaze within
we are the dust of a dying star born of the fire that burns afar
we are the ocean, we are the shore we are the answer, we are more
atoms and energy are all that we are
· · ·

the waves

you can feel the waves in everything
you can feel it all that is in you
all that is you
you can you can you can
you can feel it
feel all of it feel everything
feel it all around you feel it inside you
feel it through you
the waves through it all the waves in it all
the waves in everything the waves of the creation
the waves of the creator the waves that are you the waves that are about you
in you the waves in you everything
in all the waves in all of everything everything
is one
everything is a wave all is the same we are all
the same
atoms and energy are all that we are
· · ·

what is

what is the meaning of all of this why must we toil, why must we strain what is the point of all of this pain
why must life be lived to end in dust there must be something more than blood and bone oh this trust we have is the only must
transcend to the place from which we came and know what is, is love

Act III

Beings

i the sun

did you say that i could
be the sun to you
so warm and so bright
one truth in your life
and then yet so far from
you
the sun to you
how could i be the sun to you
when you are the sun to me
so warm and so infinite and so bright
my reason to be in this life
you are the sun
you are the sun to me as well
my universe and my infinity
how long will it be before we meet
if we could only
see the sun as one
with me next to you
but we can only see
as two
i am so far from you
· · ·

my twin

when i am near you i feel that way again
like i found my best friend like i am holding myself like i am holding my twin just the touch of you
you move me in so many ways
how would it be if we were to gaze together
as we orbit in our galaxy and throb to the waves for a time
or for an eternity
are you my twin burning together in some galaxy
in some other time
were we once two bright suns roaming the galaxy together orbiting each other
as we moved through space only to be trapped on this planet for this short time
· · ·

believe

believe in yourself believe in the power you have inside you can do anything if you believe you can let no one take this from you, it is yours
when doubt shows its face, show it away lies in the heart that believes
believe
· · ·

truth

truth be told, we never really know truth can hurt, lies they do too be true to yourself and let others be too
you see there is no universal truth what is true for you may not be true for another this you can trust, this you can know
the truth is hidden from no one it beats in every heart of everyone truth is love
· · ·

feeling again

you make me feel that feeling again like i am holding my best friend
and like you could be here to the end
you make me feel like it can be real
if i could only hold you night and day and through space and time
as we travel to some other place
if i could be there with you now
to feel you to hold you to love you
i would make the world go away
i would make the rain stay in the sky
i would turn the dark days to light
i would be there for you
and show you what love can do
the end
· · ·

dismissal — you're a star

you're a star everybody loves you
the first time they see you
you're smile you're beautiful all the way through
what you've been through
a heart of gold eyes so full of love for everyone
you're a star everybody loves you
everybody once to see you in heaven
reach out for the stars show them the love you have
they love you so much
just touch the stars and show them your heart
a heart full of gold
that's what the universe told
· · ·

transcend

transcend your body, transcend your mind transcend all space and transcend all time free yourself of all attachment to this world let your imagination fly, unfurled
there!
· · ·

return

return, return to where it all began we come from the stars and to them we’ll go
atoms and energy are all that we are and when we die we don’t go very far we stay right here on our beautiful earth becoming a part of another life’s birth
· · ·

cosmos

we are the cosmos, the cosmos are we infinite and eternal for all to see ever expanding, this space has no end the love of the cosmos has no beginning and no end
COSMOS!
